HISTORY

Misha Ivanov created the angst font in 2023, inspired by the conflict in Ukraine. Ivanov noticed a signage commemorating the friendship between Ukraine and Russia hanging in an Moscow metro station in February of 2022. This font includes two axis of variability and six basic variations.
